Implications for Optimization

  • COP optimization: Lower flow temperatures improve COP. With slope -0.2592, reducing flow by 5°C improves COP by ~1.30.
  • Timing strategy: Run heat pump during warmest outdoor temps (daytime/solar hours) for better COP. Each +1°C outdoor improves COP by ~0.203.
  • Capacity headroom: Max observed 59 kWh/day suggests capacity is sufficient for current heating demand.
  • Buffer utilization: Buffer tank (mean 36°C) provides thermal storage for load shifting.
