Thermodynamic COP Model

Theory

The thermodynamic COP model uses refrigerant pressure measurements to calculate the theoretical maximum COP (Carnot COP):

COP_carnot = T_cond / (T_cond - T_evap)    [temperatures in Kelvin]
COP_actual = η × COP_carnot                 [η is efficiency factor]

Where T_cond and T_evap are saturation temperatures derived from the high and low pressure sensors using R410A refrigerant properties.
