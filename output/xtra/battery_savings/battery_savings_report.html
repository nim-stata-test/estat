Methodology

This analysis compares the actual electricity costs with a hypothetical scenario where no battery exists:

  • With battery (actual): Grid costs based on actual imports and exports
  • Without battery (hypothetical): Battery charging energy would have been exported to the grid; battery discharging energy would have been imported from the grid

Battery savings = (Battery discharge × Purchase rate) - (Battery charge × Feed-in rate × 0.70)

The 0.70 factor accounts for the 30% income tax on feed-in revenue.

Time-varying tariffs (high/low periods) are applied at 15-minute resolution for accuracy.
