Implementation Notes

  • Curve Rise: Adjust via heat pump controller menu. Primary lever for COP improvement.
  • Schedule: Set comfort period in heat pump scheduler. Aligns heating with PV availability.
  • Setpoints: Control via Home Assistant climate entity. Automate if desired.

Success Criteria

A strategy is considered successful if:

  1. Self-sufficiency meets or exceeds prediction range
  2. COP meets or exceeds prediction range
  3. Comfort compliance ≥95% during occupied hours (08:00-22:00) (≥90% for Cost-Optimized)
  4. Cost savings meet or exceed prediction (for Cost-Optimized: ≥10% reduction)
  5. No frequent manual overrides required

Note: Night temperatures (22:00-08:00) are excluded from comfort compliance. This allows aggressive energy-saving strategies during unoccupied hours.
